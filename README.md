## 🌌 The Lore

From the furthest corners of the Cosmos, Spirits arise — each born from a unique Musical Realm, each Dimension carrying its own Space-Time signature and genre. These Spirits have mastered their music completely. Their instruments are their weapons. Their sound is their power.

For eons, Genre Gate-keepers and Purists bickered endlessly about whose music was the greatest. To settle the debate once and for all, The Gods deployed a concert stage at the very edge of the Cosmos.

This isn't just a battle for glory and legend status. **It's a battle for the soul of Music itself** — to pilot the very Destiny of the Cosmos. The Gods and History await its Victor.

---

## 🎯 Goal

**Survival of the Illest.**

Be the last Spirit standing. Knock every rival off the edge of the stage. There are no second chances — only the strongest sound survives.

---

## 🏆 How to Win

The last Spirit remaining on the stage wins. Spirits are eliminated by being knocked off the edge of the board. A Spirit with 0 Vibe left can be knocked off by even a single push — so drain your rivals' Vibe, then finish them off.

## 🎮 How to Play

### The Basics

Each turn, a Spirit has a set number of **Beats** to spend on actions. Spend them wisely.

---

### 🎵 The Five Styles

Every Spirit fights using two of the five fundamental laws of musical nature that have governed all of Time-Signature and Space since the beginning of Great Beat:

| Style | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Shred** | Blazing speed and aggression |
| **Groove** | Flow and rhythm-based dominance |
| **Flair** | Flashy and unpredictable |
| **Synth** | Calculated and long-range |
| **Edge** | Raw and relentless |

Each Spirit has a **Primary Style** (their Power / chord attack) and a **Secondary Style** (their Thrash / close-quarters attack).

---

### ⚡ Actions

#### 🚶 Move — *1 Beat*
Move up to your **Speed** stat in hexes per turn. Movement opens up positioning for attacks and amp access.

> Climbing an amp stack costs **2 movement steps** but rewards you with extended attack range and a Vibe boost on successful attacks. Nothing more confidence-boosting than rocking out on top of an amp stack.

---

#### ⚔️ Thrash Attack — *1 Beat*
Up-close and physical. Deals **1 Vibe damage** and pushes the rival **1 hex** back. Get in their face and wear them down.

> **Backstab bonus:** Hit a rival from behind for an extra push.
> **Edge Crit:** Attack a rival standing on the edge for double Vibe damage.

---

#### ⚡ Power Chord — *2 Beats*
Long-range sonic energy blasted from your instrument. Powerful — but costly. Pushes rivals back by sheer force.

A Spirit with high Vibe can absorb being blown back. But push them into a wall, an amp stack, or the edge of the board and they'll take serious Vibe damage. A Spirit at 0 Vibe **will be knocked off** by even a single push.

> **Facing matters:** Head-on attacks are partially resisted. Attacks from behind deal full push.

---

#### 🔌 Plug Into Amp — *1 Beat*
Hook up to a nearby amp stack to charge it. Only one Spirit can be plugged into an amp at a time.

#### 📢 Fire Amp — *1 Beat*
Unleash the amp's beam across the stage, blasting any rival in its path. Amps hit hard and can knock Spirits off the edge.

#### ✂️ Sabotage Amp — *Free Action*
Cut a rival's amp connection, unplugging them instantly.

---

#### 🛡️ Raise Shield — *1 Beat*
Raise a defensive shield to absorb an incoming Power Chord or Amp blast. Can't be used while standing on an amp.

---

#### 💥 Sustain Move (Ultimate) — *2 Beats*
Every successful **attack grants 2 Charge points**. Every **hit taken grants 1 Charge point**. When your **Sustain gauge is full**, unleash your Spirit's unique ultimate attack and wreak havoc on your rivals.

> Don't sit on a full Sustain gauge — a rival might knock you off before you get to use it.

---

### 💜 Vibe — Your Defense Stat

**Vibe** is your Spirit's defense and confidence. A Vibing Spirit stands tall, ready to improvise their way to the top. A Spirit whose Vibe is nearly gone can hardly stand — they're one good hit away from being knocked off the stage.

Vibe can be damaged by Thrash attacks, Power Chords, Amp blasts, and wall collisions. Climbing amp stacks and landing successful attacks while on top of one can restore Vibe.

> A Spirit at **0 Vibe** will be knocked off by the next push — no matter how small.

*Shred / Flair · Vibe: 5 · Speed: 4 · Power: 4 · Beats: 3*

Cosmic Ronin hails from a realm of the Cosmos that respects order, duty, and the sacred art of the blade. A wandering swordsman whose instrument plays like a weapon and whose weapon plays like an instrument — the two are inseparable. Its lightning-fast battojutsu shred has led many a Rival to their defeat before they even knew what had hit them — one clean draw, one clean finish.

Cosmic Ronin unleashes three devastating sonic beams across the stage, each aimed with lethal precision. Every beam that connects tears a permanent hole in the board itself — the stage shrinks with every use, and there is no coming back from the void.
